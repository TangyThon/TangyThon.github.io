No child should have to worry about cancer.

Pediatric cancer is a burden on far too many kids. Together with the central Ohio community, we are taking steps towards cancer’s end.

Our generation fights so the next doesn’t have to.

TangyThon is a team of Olentangy High School students dedicated to ending childhood cancer.

A youth philanthropy chapter of tOSU’s BuckeyeThon.

We work directly with Nationwide Children’s Hospital and Ohio State University to raise money for pediatric cancer research.

The total amount raised for Nationwide Children’s Hospital by the BuckeyeThon community in 2020.

The total amount raised for the Hematology/Oncology Unit at Nationwide Children’s Hospital by TangyThon during the 2021-22 school year.
